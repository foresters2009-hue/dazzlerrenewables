Installation-First

Built From the Roof Up

Dazzler Renewables was built from the installation side of the solar industry - not from a call center or outsourced sales pipeline.

Since 2021, our team has supported nearly 800 residential solar projects across Alberta through hands-on rooftop installation, electrical coordination, utility approvals, permitting, inspections, commissioning, and system activation.

We understand what actually happens on Alberta roofs: snow loads, attic routing, roof protection, conduit planning, electrical integration, utility requirements, and long-term system reliability.

That field experience shapes every proposal, layout, and installation we deliver.

The result is practical Alberta homeowner trust and installation confidence, not just panel, inverter, or financing promises.
